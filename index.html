My DBLP, My Researchgate, My Google Scholar

I am a researcher at the Chinese Academy of Military Science. I completed my PhD at Peking University under the supervision of Prof. Zhifang Sui. During my PhD, I worked with Prof. Baobao Chang, Prof. Sujian Li, and Dr. Jing Liu, as well as Dr. Chin-Yew Lin at Microsoft Research Asia. I received my bachelor's degree from the EECS, Peking University. My research interests include AI Safety and Security, particularly in Adversarial Examples, Interpretable Deep Learning, Model Robustness, Fairness Testing, AI Testing and Evaluation, and their applications in real-world scenarios. I serve as a reviewer for top conferences and journals such as CVPR, ECCV, ACL, EMNLP, AAAI, TIP, and Pattern Recognition.
