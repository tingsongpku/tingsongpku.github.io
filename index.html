I am a researcher at Chinese Academy of Military Science. I completed my PhD study at Peking University with Prof. Zhifang Sui. During my PhD, I work with Prof. Baobao Chang and Prof. Sujian Li, and Dr. Jing Liu, Dr. Chin-Yew Lin at Microsoft Research Asia. I received bachelor degree from EECS, Peking University.
