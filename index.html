Tingsong Jiang

Researcher

My DBLP, My ResearchGate, My Google Scholar

My research interests include AI Safety and Security, particularly in Adversarial Examples, Interpretable Deep Learning, Model Robustness, Knowledge Graph, AI Testing and Evaluation, and their applications in real-world scenarios. I serve as a reviewer for top conferences and journals such as CVPR, ECCV, ACL, EMNLP, AAAI, TIP, and Pattern Recognition.

The research group has openings for PhD students, master students, and interns, including joint training opportunities with Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Zhejiang University, Sun Yat-sen University, and University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. Email inquiries are welcome.
