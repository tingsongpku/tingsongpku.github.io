FCA

Full-Coverage Vehicle Camouflage for Multi-View Physical Adversarial Attack

FCA is the official implementation and case study of the AAAI 2022 paper FCA: Learning a 3D Full-Coverage Vehicle Camouflage for Multi-View Physical Adversarial Attack. The project focuses on generating full-coverage 3D adversarial camouflage on vehicles, making physical attacks more robust under multi-view, long-distance, and partially occluded scenarios.

The project page showcases framework overview, multi-view attack cases, partial-occlusion settings, and physical-world demonstrations, making it a strong companion to the paper instead of a code release only.

A3D

Auto Adversarial Attack and Defense Platform

A3D is a platform for searching robust neural architectures and efficient adversarial attacks, unifying automated attack and defense within one workflow. The TPAMI paper explains how searched attacks can improve robustness evaluation while searched robust architectures can in turn strengthen attack optimization.

Compared with static robustness benchmark suites, A3D emphasizes automated optimization over architectures and attack strategies, making it especially useful for systematic robustness evaluation across CIFAR10, CIFAR100, and ImageNet settings.
